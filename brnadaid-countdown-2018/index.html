Brand Aid 2018

Round Details

Inauguration and Introductory Workshop: this segment will be held on 24 June,2018. In this part of the competition the participants will be trained by the country’s top business icons, entrepreneurs and professionals about the upcoming challenges of the competition.

Online Screening: this part of the competition will also start from 24 June,2018. A team of three members will be given a topic to solve focused on a compelling business element. The solution must be based on the judgement criteria and it must include an info graph or a poster.

Round 1: On Spot Case Solving: this round will be held on 30 June, 2018. The selected teams will be instantly given a case focused on a distinct business element. They will get 1 hour to solve the case. But something astounding will be waiting for the participants in this round.

Round 2: Semifinal – It will be a surprise round.

Round 3: Grand Finale – Research Based Case: the final round will be held on 16 July, 2018. In the final round, participants will be given 3 to 4 days to solve a research based case. At the finale day of the competition, teams will have to present their ideas in front of the judge panel and have to induce the panel to win.

Grand Gala: the gala will be held on the same day of the grand finale. The revelation and achievement of the winners will be celebrated.
